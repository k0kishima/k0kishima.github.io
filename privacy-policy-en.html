Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 27, 2024

This Privacy Policy applies to the following applications provided by Fuji Enterprise:

  • Bitwise - Binary Conversion Practice App
  • Future apps provided by Fuji Enterprise

1. Information We Collect

Our apps value your privacy. They only store the following information locally on your device:

  • Game scores and statistics
  • App settings such as language preferences

All this information is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

2. How We Use Information

The collected information is used solely for:

  • Recording game progress and scores
  • Saving user preferences
  • Improving app experience

3. Information Sharing

Our apps do not share any personal information with third parties. All data is stored locally and is never transmitted externally.

4. Data Security

All data is securely stored in your device's local storage. When you uninstall the app, all local data is also removed.

5. Children's Privacy

Our apps are suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect or transmit any personal information, they are safe for children to use.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our contact form.
